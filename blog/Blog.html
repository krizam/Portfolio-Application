Reflection of First Semester

One of the highlights of my semester was getting to know my classmates and instructors. While I was initially nervous about taking classes, I was pleasantly surprised by how supportive and welcoming everyone was.From participating in group discussions to working on projects together, I enjoyed getting to know my peers and learning from their experiences. During the semester, we covered a wide range of topics related to Python programming. From basic syntax and data types to more advanced concepts like object-oriented programming and web development, I feel like I gained a solid understanding of Python and its capabilities.I also appreciated the fact that we had the opportunity to work on several projects using Python, which helped me to develop my skills and gain practical experience. In addition, I learned about batch files, Linux Ubuntu, and operating systems during my studies. Understanding the command line interface and operating systems is an important foundation for learning how to code. I also learned about number systems and their conversions, including binary, decimal, octal, and hexadecimal Overall, I had a great semester and feel like I learned a lot. From developing my technical skills to building relationships with my peers and instructors, I feel like I have grown a lot over the past few months. While there were certainly challenges along the way, I feel like I have come away from this semester with a sense of accomplishment and a renewed passion for learning.

Project Management

May 4th,2023A project is a sequence of tasks that must be completed to attain a certain outcome also we can define as a project is a temporary endeavour try undertaken to create a unique method product, service, and result. Project Management is a planning, organizing, directing, and controlling of company resources for relatively short-term objectives that has been established to complete specific goals and objectives. Project Management has been used in various field and it is need for any kind of fields. Mostly It has been used in transportation and infrastructure, IT, Building and construction, finance, and law. Project Management is important cause it paves the way for on time and on budget delivery because it lowers the chance of scope creep, budget over and employee burnout. There are various types of project management some of them are Digital Project Management, Software project Management. Software Project Management is the art of planning and leading software projects. To complete a proper project, you may need proper 2team members and must pass phases of difficulties and obstacles during management of project. There are some phases of project management in first generally, Initiation after that we need to plan for project, so next phase is planning, execution, monitoring, and control closing at the end of the project. All of these is managed by a project manager. Project manager is the one-off leads the projects from initiation stage to closing stage
